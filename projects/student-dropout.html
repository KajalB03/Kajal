Understanding Student Dropout & Academic Success Patterns

Tools: Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Power BI, KNN, Naive Bayes, K‑Means, PCA, NLP

This project analyzed a Portuguese university dataset to identify demographic, financial, and academic factors behind dropout and graduation. I enriched this with sentiment insights on public perception of higher education from YouTube comments.

Key Insights

  • Older age, male gender, and unpaid tuition were strongly associated with dropout.
  • Academic grades were the top predictors; KNN reached ~91% accuracy.
  • Clustering exposed three distinct student profiles linked to outcomes.
  • Public conversation revealed strong interest in “value vs cost” of college.

Skills Showcased

  • Data cleaning, EDA, hypothesis testing
  • Machine learning (supervised & unsupervised)
  • Public sentiment integration for contextual storytelling
