Public Opinion on Labubu Dolls — Sentiment & Topic Analysis

Tools: Python, NLTK, Scikit-learn, Gensim, Seaborn, WordCloud, NetworkX 

In 2025, Labubu dolls became a viral sensation—sparking mixed reactions. I performed NLP-driven sentiment scoring, topic modeling, and correlation analysis on YouTube comments to uncover dominant sentiments and themes.

Key Findings

  • Sentiment was largely positive/neutral; hype clusters stood out strongly.
  • Hype/fan-love were emotionally charged topics, while unboxing/cost prompted more negativity.
  • Network analysis revealed two main discourse clusters: emotional engagement vs factual reviews.

Skills Demonstrated

  • End-to-end NLP pipeline on real-world data
  • Storytelling with data visuals (word clouds, correlation networks)
  • Cluster detection and topic modeling in text
