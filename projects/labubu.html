Labubu Sentiment & Topic Analysis

Exploring public discourse on viral toy trends using NLP

Project Overview

In 2025, Labubu dolls took over TikTok, creating a mix of fan obsession and frustration. This project analyzed YouTube comment sentiment and themes using natural language processing (NLP).

🧰 Tools Used

  • Python: NLTK, Scikit-learn, Gensim
  • Visuals: Wordcloud, NetworkX, Seaborn
  • Techniques: Topic Modeling (LDA), Polarity/Subjectivity, Dendrogram Clustering

📊 Key Insights

  • Two major clusters: hype-driven emotional fans vs. critical buyers
  • Most popular topics: Fan love, blind box unboxing, frustration with pricing
  • Sentiment was largely positive, with frustration over cost and surprise boxes

🧠 Skills Demonstrated

  • Real-world NLP data cleaning and lemmatization
  • Topic correlation + clustering interpretation
  • Data storytelling through emotionally engaging visuals
